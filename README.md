# SAFESTRIDE - Smart Safety Shoes

SAFESTRIDE is an AI-powered wearable safety solution designed to improve personal safety and emergency response.

## Problem Statement

During emergencies such as harassment, accidents, or medical issues, people often cannot quickly access their mobile phones to ask for help. This delay can increase risk and reduce response time.

## Solution

We developed Smart Safety Shoes that provide real-time emergency assistance and health monitoring.

## Key Features

- SOS Emergency Alert
- GPS Live Tracking
- GSM Emergency SMS
- Heart Rate Monitoring
- Navigation Assistance

## Target Users

- Women
- Students
- Senior Citizens
- Night-Shift Workers
